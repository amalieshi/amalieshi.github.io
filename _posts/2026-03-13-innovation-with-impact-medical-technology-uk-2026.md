# 🏥 Innovation with Impact: My Day at Medical Technology UK 2026

An in-depth look at the Medical Technology UK conference at the Coventry Building Society Arena, exploring key themes in sustainability, human-centered design, and the critical balance between innovation and user experience in medical technology development.

## Key Conference Highlights

**🌱 Sustainability as Strategy**: How companies like Medtronic are transforming sustainability from regulatory burden to competitive advantage, with actionable KPIs that drive both profit and environmental impact.

**👥 Human-Centered Design**: Professor Ann Blandford's "Quality Pyramid" for health technology design, emphasizing the crucial progression from functional → usable → engaging → contextually appropriate → clinically effective.

**⚠️ Learning from Failures**: Real-world case studies from tech giants (Samsung Galaxy Note 7, Windows 8, Apple Magic Mouse 2, Google Glass, Tesla Model 3) that reveal critical pitfalls in requirements, design, engineering, validation, and manufacturing.

**🔬 Technical Innovation**: Industry updates on performance polymers, multiphysics simulation technology, and medical device biological evaluation standards.

**♀️ Women in MedTech**: Inspiring panel discussion on authentic leadership, non-linear career paths, and finding balance in engineering careers.

## Core Takeaway

> "If the user can't use it, it doesn't work."

The conference reinforced the essential shift from product-centric to human-centric design thinking in healthcare technology development.
